Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add to the map?

In order to submit your score please visit the Department of Environmental Health webpage and read about the program. Click the button that says "Enroll Now" to submit your score.

What is this all about?

This project looks to map the energy efficiency of the commercial buildings in denver that are over 5,000 square feet. If you would like to learn more please check out our about us page.
