Building an Energy Efficient Denver

The energy used in commercial buildings, apartments and condos is responsible for 57% of the greenhouse gas emissions in Denver. Together we can cut that by 20% for $1.3 billion in energy savings. The first step in improving energy efficiency is to measure it. You know the miles per gallon of your car, but do you know the 1-100 ENERGY STAR score of the buildings where you live and work?
